Our Departments

Our Medical Services

Pain Relief

Utilizing the Myofascial Release Technique, we offer effective solutions for alleviating discomfort associated with Back Pain, Sciatica, Joint Pain, and Muscle Pain.

Orthopedics Care

At our facility, we offer comprehensive orthopedic services tailored to address a range of conditions, including Arthritis, Whiplash Injury, Cervical and Lumbar Spondylosis, and Sacroiliac Joint Dysfunctions.

Paediatric

Discover comprehensive paediatric care tailored to your child's unique needs, provided by our compassionate team of experts dedicated to nurturing their health and well-being from infancy through adolescence.

Sport

At PhysioAid Clinic, our expert physiotherapists specialize in treating sports-related injuries like Tennis Elbow, Golfer's Elbow, Achilles Tendonitis, and Carpal Tunnel Syndrome.

Occupational Health

The services offered include ergonomic consulting, functional capacity/capability evaluation, job demand analysis, product design, and trainings aimed at promoting wellness and productivity in the workplace.

Consultation

Embark on your journey towards optimal wellness with our comprehensive consultation services, tailored to individuals seeking expert guidance in physiotherapy, ergonomics, and related fields.
